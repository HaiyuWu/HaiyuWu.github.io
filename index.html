About Me

Hello! I am currently working on multi-modal fusion, alignment, and reasoning of multi-modal foundation models as a Research Scientist at Altos Labs. Before this, I earned my PhD degree at the University of Notre Dame, where I worked with Prof. Kevin W. Bowyer at the Computer Vision Research Lab (CVRL).

The long-term goal of my research is to increase the transfer learning, zero-shot learning, and few-shot learning capabilities of large-scale foundation models, and to understand how models can effectively learn useful knowledge from noisy data. These motivations drive my current work on AI for biology at Altos Labs.
